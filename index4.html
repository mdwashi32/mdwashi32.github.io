Other Projects

Glitch Project - "Clouds"

The objective of this project was to use adobe photoshop and illustrator to create an image.

Afterward, I created a glitch in the image code using audacity.

The glitch is a play on the term cloud.

In a way, technology is altering the way we perceive the world around us.


MashUp Project - "Days"

The goal of this project was to mashup different art mediums together.

I chose to mashup and remix The Pharcyde - Runnin with Deadmau5 - Ghosts N Stuff.

I own the rights to neither song.


Video Project - "Distortion"

This video project was both filmed and edited by me.

The idea behind it is that we have a distorted sense of reality due to our over-reliance on smart phones and similar devices.

This distorted reality leads us to become trapped and not expand our views of the world. This is symbolized by the looping hallway.


Processing Self-Portrait Project

The objective here was to create a simple sketch of myself using processing.


Processing Video Project - "america"

In this project, I used processing to create the American flag.

The shapes bouncing around the white stripes symbolize the diversity of America:

We're all different colors, shapes, and sizes.
